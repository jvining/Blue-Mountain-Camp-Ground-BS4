Blue Mountain Campground
Family run, for family fun

Escape to the woods of the Catskill Mountains where we offer an area of open space, breathtaking beauty, and the serenity of natural mountain air. Nightly rates start at just $35. Ask about our multi-night promotions and save even more! We are open year round for full hook up rigs, perfect for a quick winter get away or whole winter stay. Hunters, skiers, and winter travelers also welcome.
